Crossbell State クロスベル自治州

The Crossbell State, formerly known as Crossbell Province, is a landlocked, self-governing demilitarised buffer state located in western Zemuria.
It is bordered by Erebonia to the west and by Calvard to the east. Located historically at this convergence point, the city has been site of an extraordinary number of battles between various groups of people.

History

Origin

With its geographical position along a route between Erebonia and Calvard, Crossbell became an important trade centre in West Zemuria. The wars from the Dark Ages continued during the Middle Ages, laying ruin to whatever had become of the city of numerous occasions. Today, Crossbell has become the test site for Epstein Foundation's Orbal Network and the heart of Zemuria's international economy.

Declaration of independence

After a brief referendum, Mayor Dieter Crois declared Crossbell's independence in S.1204 and subsequently had the IBC freeze the assets of both Erebonia and Calvard. He assumed office as president and held an inaugural speech at the Orchis Tower. Immediately after the speech, the 5th Armored Division of Erebonia's Imperial Army and Calvard's Republic Air Force invaded Crossbell, only to meet complete annihilation through the means of Ouroboros developed Aions. In the process, Garrelia Fortress was wiped out by Aion Type-α, powered by the Sept-Terrion of Zero.

Imperial occupation

Crossbell's independence did not last for long. The Erebonian Civil War came to an end early January S.1205 with Chancellor Giliath Osborne gaining full support of the fallen Noble Alliance, leading to an unexpected blitzkrieg invasion of Crossbell. Within a single day and without bloodshed, its capital fell in the hands of Erebonia. On 14 January S.1205, recently re-appointed Mayor Henry MacDowell signed a treaty with Erebonia and forfeited Crossbell's autonomy; the state became Erebonia's Crossbell Province once again. In February that year, newly appointed Governor Rufus Albarea announced that Crossbell would merge with Erebonia.
The Imperial occupation lasted until S.1207.

Tio Plato

  • Age: 16
  • Weapon: Orbal Staff
  • Affiliation: Special Support Section Inspector; Epstein Foundation Engineering Chief;

Tio is a small and reserved girl. She is very polite at first meeting, but warms up overtime and gives a bit of subtle sass and jokes. Tio loves all things cute, especially Mishy, but does not like to admit it. Little is known about her past, but she worked as part of the Foundation's research team since early childhood due to her being a genius. After reaching 14, she was transferred to Crossbell State in exchange to establish the Police's newest division, the Special Support Section. In truth, she enrolled as Epstein Foundation's tester for the new Orbal Staffs while researching on the "Eion System", a link system which is said to enable the “resonance connection of info-processing” between humans and machines.
